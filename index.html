About us

Cusana Insights is an intelligence platform for marketers and salespeople to better understand customers and measure their KPIs on social media.

With the wealth of insights we provide, companies can improve relationships with customers as we analyze information beyond psychographics to leverage on the customers needs, interests and wants.

We help Small and Medium enterprises achieve a deep level of emotional connections with the people they are trying to reach in a reputable, predictable and scalable fashion.

This helps businesses understand who each audience segment is, what they are influenced by, what they talk about, share and utimately care most about.
